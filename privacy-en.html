PurrGo Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

Welcome to PurrGo ("the App"). We value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, used for registration and sign-in.
  • Task Data: Goals and tasks you create, stored under your personal account.
  • AI Interaction Data: Task descriptions you enter are sent to our AI service to generate task breakdown suggestions.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect device location data
  • We do not collect contacts or address books
  • We do not collect photos or camera data
  • We do not include any advertising trackers
  • We do not sell any user data to third parties

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is hosted and stored through Tencent CloudBase, encrypted in transit via SSL. Tencent Cloud complies with Level 3 information security protection standards.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Tencent CloudBase: Provides user authentication and data storage services.
  • AI Service: Powers the intelligent task breakdown feature.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You may delete your account and data at any time within the App. After account deletion, all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please email: purrgo.app@outlook.com
